Analytics

We analyze your site's data so you don't have to.

We use modern analyzing tools to make and keep your users' experience fresh.

Our custom analytics tools show us your web traffic, bounce rate, conversation rate, how people are using your website, when they leave, etc. We manually sort through these for you and send you a report every 3 days with a summary and our advice. Our reports have a button you can click to accept our advice and we will make the changes happen.

We also give you access to the analytics panel to do your own report generation and give you the ability to integrate tools you may already be familiar with such as (but not limited to) Google Analytics, Mixpanel, and Visual Website Optimizer.
